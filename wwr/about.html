Mount Elbert Rafting

Mount Elbert rafting is a dedicated white water rafting company that has been running since 2012. We are deedicated to providing you and your family with an exciting, safe and beautiful experience in the great outdoors!

History

We have been operating sincw 2012. We are family owned and operated amongst 3 siblings who love the outdoors. Growing up all three of us spent most of our time outdoors, including white water rafting. Every summer our family took a weekend trip to Colorado Springs for a day long white waqter rafting trip! This was something we looked forward to every year as it brought us much excitement.

Now, 12 years later, we hope to provide the same expereince from families across the country. As families and loved ones gather for this exciting adventure, our hope is to provide you with a safe and adventerous journey thorugh the mountains.

Our Mission

At Life Adventures, Our mission is to provide safe and radical moments for our clients, so that we do not miss any unique moment of the experiences we have along the way in our lives.

Adventure Awaits You!

Whether you're a first-timer or an experienced rafter, we have the perfect adventure waiting for you. From calm water trips to challenging rapids, every journey with us is unique and exciting. Bring all your friends and come have a unique rafting adventure at Life Adventures. Here you will enjoy beautiful waterfalls with great spots to have your meals and rest, you will also have the chance to have one of the most beautiful views of the entire valley.
